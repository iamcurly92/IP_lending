Fundamentals of Manual Testing

Looking to enter the IT industry and become a master in software testing? Join our Kseniia's QA Fundamentals of Manual Testing course.

We conduct the Fundamentals of Manual Testing course each year in spring and autumn. The course is designed for those with little or no professional IT experience and non-IT professionals looking to enter the IT industry. Testing professionals provide insight into the latest software testing technology, market requirements, and best practice testing processes, starting with the basics and building up to advanced testing techniques.

Mobile Application Testing

The Mobile Application Testing certification is intended for professionals who are working with mobile technology.

We provide an insight into methods, techniques, and tools a professional may use to test mobile applications. It covers the required mobile project activities, roles, methods, and methodologies.
